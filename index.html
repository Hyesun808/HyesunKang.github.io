Exploratory Data Analysis in SQL

In this project, I have explored a year's worth of sales data from a fictitious pizza store. The raw data included information on time, date and order details of each order and types, price, and ingredients of pizzas sold. I was able to derive information on customer behavior (such as typical number of pizzas per order, peak time, day, and month), revenue details (such as monthy revenue generated by each pizza type), and popular ingredients for each category (i.e., 5 most popular Meat Toppings).
